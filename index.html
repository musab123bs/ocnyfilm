Historic & Breathtaking Locations Just Outside New York City.

Browse Fliming Location

Orange County is one of the most beautiful counties in New York. Approximately 60 miles north of NYC, Orange County offers everything from urban landscapes, historic architecture, beautiful farmland, rolling hills, picturesque villages, tranquil lakes, quaint main streets, the majestic Hudson River on our eastern border, and the beautiful Delaware River on our western border. In addition to scenic locations, we have properties across the county, such as empty school buildings, office buildings, old psychiatric centers and former camps – all waiting to be featured in YOUR next film.
